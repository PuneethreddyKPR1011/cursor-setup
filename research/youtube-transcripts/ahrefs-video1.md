# Ahrefs - Keyword Research Tutorial for SEO

Video Link: https://youtu.be/OMJQPqG2Uas?si=yLuLiKqWrGnhO0lS

## Summary
This video explains how to perform keyword research by focusing on search intent, business value, and customer conversion rather than just search volume or traffic.

## Key Insights
- Keyword research should focus on revenue, not just traffic
- Search intent is critical (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional)
- Informational content helps build trust and guide users through the funnel
- Business value of keywords is more important than search volume
- Competitor analysis helps discover high-performing topics

## Strategy Takeaways
- Target keywords across all stages of the customer journey
- Focus on keywords that can convert into customers
- Analyze top-ranking pages before creating content
- Use internal linking to move users toward conversion
- Prioritize topics where your product naturally fits

## Why This Video Matters
This video provides a data-driven approach to content strategy, which is essential for building scalable B2B SaaS marketing systems.
