# SaaStr - Workshop on B2B SaaS Growth Strategies

Video Link: https://youtu.be/lK6KDTuMK0U?si=nel_rEGFB2eoK5-q

## Summary
This workshop explains practical strategies for growing a B2B SaaS company by adding new growth layers such as outbound sales, product expansion, customer events, and global expansion.

## Key Insights
- Growth comes from adding new layers, not just improving existing ones
- High-quality outbound still works if personalized and targeted
- Customer events are powerful for converting prospects
- Multi-product strategy is critical for long-term scaling
- Enterprise customers hold the largest budgets, especially for AI products

## Strategy Takeaways
- Add at least one new growth channel each year
- Use highly personalized outbound instead of mass emails
- Organize small customer events to drive conversions
- Build additional products before growth slows down
- Focus on enterprise expansion to capture higher-value customers

## Why This Video Matters
This video provides real-world SaaS growth strategies used by successful companies and founders, making it highly relevant for building a B2B content strategy playbook.
